Why Go To Seminary:

  • Mature as a believer in Christ

    Cultivate a greater maturity in your Christian walk through a deeper, more intimate relationship with the Lord Jesus Christ.

  • Set a foundation in biblical hermeneutics

    Learn the skill of accurately interpreting the Scriptures on your own.

  • Understand and apply theology

    Understand and articulate a systematic theological position and its intricacies in both the OT and NT.

  • Apply your knowledge as you learn

    Develop both men and women as leaders in the local church, parachurch and Christian community.

About Phoenix Seminary

Phoenix Seminary is a nationally-recognized, graduate level theological seminary located in the heart of Phoenix, Arizona. This centralized metropolitan location provides our students with a wide range of multi-cultural and multi-generational opportunities to gain practical ministry experience. Phoenix Seminary is where hundreds of men and women have embarked upon the most important journey of their lives.
